About Us

We're so glad you found Sketchbook Rainbow - a place for kids of all ages to connect with their creative spirits through the simple joy of coloring! Here at Sketchbook Rainbow, we believe that coloring pages are more than just a fun pastime. The slow, methodical act of filling in shapes and staying within the lines can help improve children's fine motor skills, hand-eye coordination, creativity and concentration. But most of all, we hope coloring provides an escape for kids and adults alike - a moment out of time to simply relax, have fun and unleash their imagination. As adults, many of us fondly remember the hours we spent bringing drawings to life with our crayons.

That's why we created this website - to share our love of coloring with the next generation. Our growing library of free printable coloring pages features a wide range of themes sure to spark the creativity of any budding artist. From cute animals and magical fantasy scenes to educational topics and pop culture characters, there is something for every interest. We work hard to provide high-quality coloring pages that are fun, educational and offer just the right amount of challenge. Our pages are clearly drawn with wide lines so little ones don't get frustrated, but include enough detail to engage older children and adults. So grab your favorite set of markers or crayons, pull up a chair and start coloring! We hope Sketchbook Rainbow provides you and your child with many hours of simple, carefree fun. Let your imagination run wild and enjoy the journey. We look forward to bringing you new coloring pages regularly to keep the fun coming. If you have any suggestions for how we can make Sketchbook Rainbow even better, please let us know. We love hearing from our creative community!

Happy Coloring,
The Sketchbook Rainbow Team
